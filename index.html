About Me

Hello, my name is Nichole Holmstedt. I am a recent graduate from Bellevue University with my Bachelors in Software Development. I currently work as a claims processor for the Veterans Affairs, but I am hoping soon that I can move into the Information Technology field!

I fell in love with building websites and coding in 2018 when I chose to go into the Software Development degree. Ever since then, I have been working on random website projects. One website I built was for Dungeons and Dragons while another website I built was for my sisters blog with her ghost hunting friends. Unfortunately, the website I built for my sister is taken down for now since they are going through issues and don't know if they want to continue it. I really do enjoy building them though. I build all my websites from scratch using HTML and CSS.

I recently moved to Colorado Springs, Colorado in 2020 right before COVID hit. I usually spend my free time going on hikes, walks, paddleboarding, or playing video games. Of course thats all when I am not coding. I am hoping to build more this year and can't wait to see more growth in myself.
